Additional Achievements & Trivia

Beyond my formal credentials, I also possess a remarkable memory (which I like to refer to as eidetic). I've authored several papers on string theory (available upon request), regularly engage in high-level physics symposiums, and conduct occasional lectures that enthrall my colleagues. (Or so I'd like to think—some “lesser minds” find my lectures intimidating rather than illuminating.)

Additionally, I am proud to say that I've never missed “Halo Night” since its inception, except in rare instances of illness—though thanks to my immune system, even microbes tend to avoid me.

Many have asked if I aspire to a Nobel Prize. The short answer: “Does the Earth revolve around the Sun?” (Yes, it does, and yes, I will get one.)

My Spot on the Couch

You see that seat cushion? That is my spot. It’s located in the perfect position relative to the television and cross-breeze of the window, minimizing both glare and drafts. Visitors are strongly advised not to sit there under any circumstances. You’ve been warned.

Personal Tidbits

Despite my unparalleled intellect, some people find my social skills to be... challenging. I maintain a series of highly specific Roommate Agreements, much to my roommate Leonard’s dismay, and I adhere to a strict personal schedule that includes a designated laundry night (Saturday at 8:15 PM) and a bedtime no later than 10:00 PM (except under extreme circumstances, such as live NASA broadcasts).

Should you ever meet my mother, Mary Cooper, don’t mention evolution. She might take it personally. And if I ever knock on your door, expect at least three knocks followed by the repeated invocation of your name—this is non-negotiable.
