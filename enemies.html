Those Who Oppose the Great Dr. Cooper

Lest anyone think me petty, please note that this list is based entirely on objective analysis and rigorous observation. These individuals have committed trespasses so egregious, they cannot be forgiven.

My List of Nemeses

  1. Wil Wheaton – He betrayed me in 1995 by failing to attend a Star Trek convention, thus shattering my dreams of meeting Wesley Crusher.
  2. Barry Kripke – His constant ridicule of my work (and that speech impediment—“Hewo, Shewdon”) does not amuse me.
  3. Leslie Winkle – She dared to mock string theory and called me a “dumbass.” Unforgivable!
  4. Brent Spiner – He opened a signed, mint-condition Wesley Crusher action figure in front of me. A betrayal of collectible integrity!
  5. Howard Wolowitz (Occasionally) – He’s not a real doctor. That is all.
  6. Steve Jobs (Posthumously) – Overpriced Apple products. Need I say more?
  7. Billy Sparks – My childhood bully from Texas who tormented me for years. Some scars never heal.
  8. Mystery Slot – A rotating “New Nemesis of the Week.” Because there’s always room on the list for fresh offenses.

If you believe you’ve wronged me enough to join this esteemed list, please submit a formal request for consideration via my homepage. I’ll evaluate your transgression in due time.
